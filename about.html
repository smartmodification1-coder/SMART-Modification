Our Story

Founded in 2015, Smart Modification started as a small garage with a big dream: to bring world-class vehicle customization to Sri Lanka. We saw a gap in the market for high-quality parts and professional installation services that enthusiasts could trust.

Today, we are the leading destination for automotive upgrades, serving thousands of happy customers. From simple aesthetic tweaks to full performance overhauls, our team of expert technicians treats every car as if it were their own.
